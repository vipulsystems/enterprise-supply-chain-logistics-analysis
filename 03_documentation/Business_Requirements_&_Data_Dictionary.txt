1. PROJECT OBJECTIVE & PROBLEM STATEMENT
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The objective of this project is to build an end-to-end Executive Supply Chain Analytics platform for a global e-commerce enterprise handling 100,000+ annual orders. The management wants to optimize operations by identifying delivery bottlenecks, reducing high shipping costs, detecting product damage risks, and tracking warehouse inventory health to avoid stock-outs.

The project answers critical business questions across three main categories:

Page 1: Executive Financials & Shipping Cost Trends

Page 2: Logistics Carrier SLAs, Delays, and Root-Cause Analysis

Page 3: Warehouse Capacity, Inventory Risks, and Supplier Reliability


2. KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS (KPIs) & BUSINESS LOGIC
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
To measure success, the dashboard implements the following corporate metrics via DAX:

* On-Time Delivery (OTD %):
  Formula = (Total On-Time Orders / Total Orders) * 100
  Target Benchmark = 85% or higher.

* Net Profit:
  Formula = Revenue - (Item Cost + Shipping Cost)

* Net Profit Margin %:
  Formula = (Total Net Profit / Total Item Revenue) * 100

* Product Damage Rate %:
  Formula = (Count of Damaged Items / Total Orders Received) * 100

* Order Return Rate %:
  Formula = (Count of Returned Items / Total Orders Received) * 100


3. DATA DICTIONARY (CRITICAL METRICS EXPLAINED)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The database contains 45 attributes. Below are the key operational definitions:

OrderID: Unique transaction key for each customer order.

IsDelayed (Yes/No): Flag identifying if Actual Delivery Time exceeded Expected Time.

DelayReason: Root cause of delay (Severe Weather, Customs Delay, Traffic, etc.).

IsDamaged (Yes/No): Flag indicating if the product was damaged during transit.

ReturnStatus (Returned/None): Captures reverse logistics data for customer returns.

WarehouseInventoryLevel: Current stock units available in the specific hub.

SafetyStockLevel: Mini threshold req to prevent stockout. If Inventory < SafetyStock, it triggers a "HighRisk" alert.

SupplierReliabilityScore: Quality rating of the manufacturer (Scale 0 to 1).


4. ARCHITECTURE & TARGET AUDIENCE
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Data Model: Star Schema Design (1 Fact Table, 5 Dimension Tables)

Row Scale: 100,000+ transactional records.

Audience: Chief Operations Officer (COO), Logistics Directors, Regional Warehouse Managers.
